WE TAKE CARE OF EVERYTHING

IT Farm's cloud services give you secure, compliant and resilient IT without the usual worry about what's happening behind the scenes. Your staff can access their data, files and applications from any device, at any time.

But, even better, IT Farm take care of your data protection, backups, upgrades and support, allowing your staff to be more productive and freeing you up to focus on your business.

Our comprehensive cloud services include:

Hosted Desktop

Your traditional PC desktop, securely accessed on the cloud, from anywhere, on any device. Also known as Desktop as a Service (DaaS), Hosted Desktop gives you the same look and feel as your PC desktop, but your applications, files and data are securely located in our state of the art data centres.

Hosted Applicatios

Your Windows applications, run securely on the cloud. Also known as Software as a Service (SaaS), Hosted Applications are your everyday Windows applications on the cloud as opposed to installing them on your local PC.

Hosted Telephony

By upgrading from a traditional telephone exchange to a hosted telephony system, you'll introduce the cloud across your call and teleconferencing tasks too. We'll even supply your physical handsets if your hardware is due for replacement.
